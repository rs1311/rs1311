interests


Math

From a young age, I have had an extremely profound interest in mathematics. To me, mathematics was a tool that people could harness to convey ideas regardless of languages and differences. Mathematics is also a field that allows one to explore the intangible, and is not constrained by the physical plane of existence. In mathematics, I find certain concepts to be interesting to learn about, such as imaginary numbers, infinite sequences and paradoxes. I have begun to learn about these certain things at my own pace. When I was in Year 2, I began my reasearch into calculus as I thought that it was an intriguing concept to learn. In Year 3, I began to learn about ideas that meddle with infinity, such as Grandi's summation and Ramanujan's summation. Soon, I wish to begin learning about the Taylor series and Zeta function.

Physics

Physics has also been an area of great interest for me. Ever since we learnt about circuits in primary 4, I began to cultivate a love for physics. In year 2, a few of my friends and I banded together to create an interest group for learning about the wonders of quantum physics and relativity. This group, headed by one of our teachers at the time, helped me to explore the Universe in more ways than I thought was possible.

Cryptography

I spend a portion of my free time playing online computer games, such as Warframe, Splitgate, Minecraft and a few others. In these games, I am able to interact with people from all over the world, while forging friendships and having fun. Although I do take much joy in playing these games, I do ensure that I take breaks so that I can remain productive.

Taekwondo

Taekwondo is a martial art that has significantly impacted my life. I have been going to lessons from a very young age, initially in the pursuit of fitness. However, over the years, I have gained much more benefit from it in terms of discipline. I have learnt about pushing myself to my limits and staying committed to training. I have also learnt that control and mental fortitude is important in every action we take. Taekwondo has imparted good values in me, such as determination, resilience and team spirit.
